🎯 How to Use (For Agents)
T1
Strong Credit / Prime Client
Confidently mention our Tier 1 lenders — LightStream, SoFi, Upstart, Axos, LendingClub, Prosper. These names build trust and set the right expectation early.
T2
Average Credit / Mixed Profile
Position Mid-Tier lenders — Best Egg, Avant, Upgrade, Reprise Financial, Splash. Let them know we have flexible options designed for their situation.
T3
Challenged Credit / Credit Rebuild
Reassure client we work with all credit profiles. Guide toward Subprime lenders — Happy Money, OneMain, Mariner, NetCredit, OppLoans, RISE. We have options built for their situation.
💡 KEY: Match the client with the right expectation early using real lender names. This builds credibility and confidence throughout the call. Be comfortable referencing these names naturally in conversation.
🚫 DO NOT WORK THE FOLLOWING LEADS:
Ineligible States: Wisconsin, Oregon, Minnesota
Ineligible Clients: Under age, no SSN or ITIN, under bankruptcy within the last 2 years (must be discharged), Unworkable files, unsecured debt below $10K, no source of income
💡 USE WHEN ASKED: "We are private lenders — we work with several lending institutions such as SoFi, OneMain, LightStream, just to mention a few. The difference between a bank and us is that we have lower interest rates."
