⚙️ Rebuttal Framework
Use for every objection — 4-step flow.
A
ACKNOWLEDGE — Validate the concern.
"I understand, [Name]""Thanks for asking""I'm glad you mentioned that"
S
SOFTEN — Show empathy, don't fight it.
"Most clients had the same question""Totally valid point"
E
EXPLAIN — Give the direct rebuttal. Answer the concern.
P
PIVOT — Get back into the script with a question.
"So going back…""Let me get that confirmed…""Let's see what you qualify for…"
⚡ RULE: Always end a rebuttal with a qualifying question to keep moving toward transfer.
🔄 FLOW EXAMPLE
Client: "I don't want to give my SSN."
A → "Completely understand."  |  S → "Most people ask this."  |  E → "It's a soft pull, won't affect your score."  |  P → "Go ahead with your social whenever you're ready. [WAIT]"
