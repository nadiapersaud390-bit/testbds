🎙️ SPEAKING PACE
TOO FAST ❌
They zone out or get suspicious.
JUST RIGHT ✅
Calm, clear, confident.
TOO SLOW ⚠️
Sounds unsure. Busy people hang up.
Speak like you're explaining something important to a sharp friend — not reading a script.
⏸️ THE PAUSE — YOUR MOST POWERFUL TOOL
Pause AFTER your punch word — not before.

WRONG: "I want to talk to you about… working capital."
RIGHT: "We can get you access to working capital. [PAUSE] Does that interest you?"

Silence after a strong word = confidence.
👊 PUNCH WORDS — HIT THESE WITH WEIGHT
Slow down slightly on punch words. Drop your voice a touch — heavier, not louder. Pause 1 beat after.
🧠 MINDSET
✅ Speak clearly, calmly — they're busy, make it easy to follow
✅ Sit up straight — posture affects your voice; they hear it
✅ Smile while you talk — they feel it, they can't see it
⚠️ No filler words — no "um", "like" — silence is better
⚠️ Ask a question — then WAIT. First to speak loses.
🔄 MICRO-TRUST PHRASES — USE EVERY 2 QUESTIONS
"Perfect, thank you."
"Got it, that helps."
"Great to know."
"Appreciate that."
